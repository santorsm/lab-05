About Me

I'm a former USAF instructor pilot and investment management professional. Flying jet airplanes was a dream, executing missions all over the world. I took that leadership training to managing and trading multi-million (at times billions) dollar portfolios - nearly as exciting and detail-oriented as flying.
I learned a lot during those days, and honed my leadership skills in highly stressful and dynamic situations. Both involved mixing man & highly technical machines to deliver solutions to clients; this is just the next step and a more creative at that.
